The Track

"Nokia" is a song by Canadian rapper Drake from his collaborative studio album with PartyNextDoor entitled "Some Sexy Songs 4 U". Written by Drake and produced by Paul Omar "Elkan" Agyei, it was released on February 14, 2025, by Santa Anna Label Group, OVO Sound and Republic Records.

Our Approach

  • Distributed 30,000 posts in just 1 week across TikTok and YouTube Shorts

  • Targeted key markets in North America, UK, Australia and New Zealand
  • Coordinated massive push around the single release

"Nokia" peaked at number five on the Canadian Hot 100 and number two on the Billboard Hot 100 on April 19, 2025. Outside of North America, "Nokia" peaked within the top ten of the charts in Australia, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom. It impacted contemporary hit radio as the album's second single on March 11, 2025.
